Why Computer Science?

I was accepted to the University at Buffalo as an Aerospace Engineering major, but after a little thinking I decided to start my college career as a Computer Science major. There are two main reason behind my decision. The first reason is my interest in the subject. My father is a software engineer and ever since I was a little kid, seeing the work he has done has always interested me. I would love to be doing what he has been doing for his whole life. The second reason is the amount of job opportunities. Computers are our future, and I would like to be a part of the development of our future.
